This article explains how to switch between the different map layers on the platform. There are three distinct map layers available for you to choose from:

- Mapbox Street
- Mapbox Dark
- Mapbox Satellite 

Select the <span style={{ display: 'inline-block' }}><Icons.Picker height="1.125rem" width="1.125rem" /></span> button at the bottom right of the screen, to view the available map layers. Click to toggle between the different map layers, and the map will update instantly to reflect your selection.
