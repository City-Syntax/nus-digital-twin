This article guides you on how to capture a screenshot on the NUS Digital Twin platform.

Select the <span style={{ display: 'inline-block' }}><Icons.Camera height="1.125rem" width="1.125rem" /></span> button at the bottom left of the screen. A screenshot of the current view without the menus will be generated, and saved to your device.
