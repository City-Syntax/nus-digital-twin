This article gives information on how to use the solar controls on the platform. This control provides solar information about the area surrounding the campus.

## Viewing Solar

Click on the <span style={{ display: 'inline-block' }}><Icons.Solar height="1.125rem" width="1.125rem" /></span> Solar button on the menu on the right. If your menu appears on the bottom, select the <span style={{ display: 'inline-block' }}><Icons.Solar height="1.125rem" width="1.125rem" /></span> button to locate the Solar button.
