This article provides guidance on how to utilise the wind controls on the platform. This control provides wind information about the area surrounding the campus.

## Viewing Wind

Click on the <span style={{ display: 'inline-block' }}><Icons.Wind height="1.125rem" width="1.125rem" /></span> Wind button on the menu on the right. If your menu appears on the bottom, select the <span style={{ display: 'inline-block' }}><Icons.Controls height="1.125rem" width="1.125rem" /></span> button to locate the Wind button.
