This article provides information on how to use the thermal comfort controls on the platform. This control provide information on the thermal comfort of the area surrounding the campus, such as the University Thermal Comfort Index.

## Viewing Thermal Comfort

Click on the <span style={{ display: 'inline-block' }}><Icons.ThermalComfort height="1.125rem" width="1.125rem" /></span> Thermal Comfort button on the menu on the right. If your menu appears on the bottom, select the <span style={{ display: 'inline-block' }}><Icons.Controls height="1.125rem" width="1.125rem" /></span> button to locate the Thermal Comfort button.
